Countering Extremist Fake News

Extremists burning churches?

Are Buddhist Extremists in India Burning Churches? A widely circulated Whatsapp message asks people in Olisabang to come out and protect their churches or else Buddhist Extremists will burn them at night. This news article is completely fake and is used to spred propogandist material online. Click here to know more about this incident and how it came under our check.

Beheading Robot Citizen?

A claim surfaced online recently saying that an angry mob in Saudi Arabia beheaded "Sophia," a robot that was recently awarded citizenship. When digged deep, the claim came from a local satirical website, and hence was false in a complete sense. However it did quite a damage to the nation's integrity and many clashes online. Click here to know more about this incident.

Unverified Tweets?

Turkish Deputy PM, Mehmet Simsek tweeted pictures on Rakhine Conflict regarding Rohingya issue in Myanmar, urging international committee to wake up and help the troubled. But, the images were completely unrelated to the Rakhine Conflict, thereby the Deputy PM being denounced on Twitter for the mis-information that he had spread. Click here to see how his tweet was examined and determined to be false.

Video of a Mob Lynching?

A Facebook post of a class 11 student showing a Hindu mob lynching a Muslim man became viral and sparked communal tensions in Basirhat, WB. Angry people burnt shops, buses and even killed a man, except for doing the most logical thing, verifying the source. The video cam out to be of a completely unrelated 2 year old incident in Pakistan that caused such damage now. Click here to see the complete story of this video.
